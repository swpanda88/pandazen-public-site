Booking Terms

Last updated: July 2026

1. Quotes and Availability

Any initial quote provided via email, phone, or website form is an estimate based on the information provided. The final scope and price will be confirmed prior to or during the first visit. There is no guarantee of availability until a booking is explicitly confirmed by Panda Zen. We reserve the right to decline unsuitable work.

2. Access and Preparations

Clear access to the property must be arranged prior to our arrival. Clients must disclose any relevant issues regarding parking, difficult access, or pets before the booking is confirmed. We request that spaces are reasonably tidied prior to our arrival so we can focus our time on thorough cleaning.

3. Cancellations and Rescheduling

We require at least 48 hours' notice for any cancellations or requests to reschedule a clean. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee.

4. Limitations of Service

While we strive for the highest standards, there are practical limits to domestic cleaning. We cannot guarantee the complete removal of deep stains, ingrained mould, or heavy limescale build-up. We will not clean unsafe areas (e.g., biohazards, areas requiring tall ladders) and request that clients notify us of any highly delicate surfaces or pre-existing damage before work commences.

5. Payment Terms

Payment terms will be agreed upon confirmation of your booking. Payments are typically handled via bank transfer upon completion of the work or on an agreed billing cycle for regular clients.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding these terms, please contact us at hello.pandazen@gmail.com.
