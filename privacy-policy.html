Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Panda Zen ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you when you submit an enquiry through our website, including:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name.
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone numbers, area/postcode.
  • Service Data: Details about your home (size, bedrooms, bathrooms, pets, parking) and cleaning requirements.
  • Marketing Data: Your preferences in receiving marketing from us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a quote or service estimate.
  • To manage our relationship with you, including notifying you about changes to our terms or privacy policy.
  • To send you occasional updates or offers if you have opted in to receive marketing communications.

4. How Enquiries Are Handled

When you submit a quote request via our website form, the information is processed securely and sent directly to us as an email notification. We use this email to assess your cleaning needs and follow up with you. We do not store this data in a separate online database.

5. Data Sharing

Your information is used by Panda Zen only. We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers (such as secure email delivery services like Resend) solely for the purpose of operating our business and communicating with you.

6. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, regulatory, tax, accounting or reporting requirements. Enquiry emails that do not lead to a booking are typically kept for a limited time for administrative purposes before being securely deleted.

7. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we are relying on consent to process your personal data.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: hello.pandazen@gmail.com.
